Recent Project: BlastOff

Our team participated in a Designathon where we developed the BlastOff App, an innovative solution for the emerging space tourism industry. The app is designed to streamline and enhance the journey for passengers embarking on space tourism adventures, with a focus on travel to Mars.

The BlastOff App covers all aspects of the travelers' experience, from organizing travel details and booking space travel tickets to arranging accommodations, scheduling training sessions, and even securing spots for Martian exploration.

Given the potential rise in visitors, the app efficiently manages the complexities of space travel logistics while prioritizing safety, accessibility, and convenience. Our challenge was to develop an app that comprehensively supports all stages of Mars space tourism, providing an engaging and user-friendly experience.
